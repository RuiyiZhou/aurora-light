Privacy
Policy.

Effective date: April 27, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 27, 2026

Aurora Light ("the app") is a personal aurora-forecasting utility built to help you find and observe the northern and southern lights. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Aurora Light may request access to your device's location in order to calculate aurora visibility at your current latitude. Specifically:

  • Approximate location — used to estimate your distance from the auroral oval and display a visibility verdict (e.g. "Possible" or "Excellent").
  • Precise location — optionally used when a more accurate latitude is needed for your forecast.

Location data is used exclusively on-device to compute visibility results. It is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server operated by Aurora Light.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports beyond what the operating system provides
  • Any information about your contacts, photos, or other apps

Third-Party Services

Aurora Light fetches space-weather data from the NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC) public API (swpc.noaa.gov). These are unauthenticated public endpoints — no personal data is sent with these requests. NOAA's own privacy policy applies to their servers.

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics libraries, or social login providers.

Data Retention

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing to retain or delete. Location access is requested at runtime and used only for the duration of the calculation. No location history is written to disk.

Children's Privacy

Aurora Light is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
